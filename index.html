Butterflies

Some of the most beautiful transformations we get to witness in nature is that of a butterfly. There are so many around the world to discover.

To learn more about the butterfly species visit Wiki Butterfly!

Life Cycle

Butterflies experience a four-stage insect life cycle.

  • Egg
  • Caterpillar larva
  • Pupa
  • Adult

I was an egg.

Behavior

Food

Butterflies feed primarily on nectar from flowers.

Other forms of nourishment are:

  1. Pollen
  2. Tree sap
  3. Rotting fruit
  4. Dung
  5. Decaying flesh
  6. Dissolved minerals in wet sand or dirt

Mating

Some male species seek hilltops and ridge tops in search of females for mating.
